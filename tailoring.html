ONE STOP CLEANERS

Exceptional and precise tailoring!

One Stop Cleaners offers full tailoring services by master tailors with decades of experience. Everything from a simple hem to a reconstruction of a garment is provided at an affordable rate.
In addition to providing all types of tailoring on clothing, we also provide alterations on home furnishing such as drapes, curtains, rugs, and more. We guarantee your satisfaction with our services!


Count on us for all your tailoring, hemming, and alteration needs and you'll get nothing less than outstanding tailoring services to help you look your best. We also perform fittings to ensure the utmost satisfaction with the alterations.

Services include:
  • Hem dresses
  • Taper pants and jackets
  • Repair tears
  • Replace zippers on any article of clothing
  • Shorten sleeves
  • Sew patches
